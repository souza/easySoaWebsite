EasySOA FUI10 collaborative project

After being labelized by the System@tic competitive cluster for Open Source, the EasySOA project was selected to receive public funding in the context of the French FUI10 call to project, another alumni being the Compatible One sister project, that focuses on cloud interoperability.

Project leader: Open Wide
Start date: November 01, 2010
Finish date: November 01, 2012

Total amount: 4 024 K€
Funders: Oseo, Region Ile de France (1 938 K€ total)
Partners: INRIA, Nuxeo, Talend, Bull, Open Wide
Sponsors: System@tic Paris, Oseo
